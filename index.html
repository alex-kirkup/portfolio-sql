About me

I have been Lead Data Analyst at a thriving secondary school in Fareham, Hampshire since 2019, and Teacher of Mathematics and Further Mathematics since 2015.

I have transitioned the school towards using pupil data intelligently to make more effective decisions and interventions, improving outcomes for pupils, parents and staff.

See my portfolio for the various solutions I have implemented to improve the school's outcomes using data.

My previous role at the school was Pupil Premium Coordinator from 2017-19, responsible for making most effective use of our budget for disadvantaged pupils and those from service families, and reporting on this to statutary bodies.

Before becoming a teacher of mathematics I was awarded a PhD in Global Political Economy from the University of Southampton (2009), which included MPhil research methods and statistical analysis, and lectured and supervised candidates at the University of Southampton and at the University of Sheffield in BSc and MSc courses in Politics, International Relations and International Political Economy.
